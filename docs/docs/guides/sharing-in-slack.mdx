## Adding Slack to your organization (Lightdash Cloud)

Here's a two minute video showing you how to install and configure the Lightdash Slack integration:

<div style="position: relative; padding-bottom: 64.98194945848375%; height: 0;"><iframe src="https://www.loom.com/embed/9df1e9e24ead48cd8e46a73558d31764?sid=1daa03c8-c783-4f46-ad6e-8dabbd7d5223" frameborder="0" webkitallowfullscreen mozallowfullscreen allowfullscreen style="position: absolute; top: 0; left: 0; width: 100%; height: 100%;"></iframe></div>

If you want to give slack access to your Lightdash organization, go to `Settings > Integrations` and click `Add to Slack` button.

<img
  src={SlackSettings}
  width="1338"
  height="659"
  style={{ display: 'block', margin: '0 auto 20px auto' }}
/>

This will redirect you to an OAUTH page to allow our App to access your Organization.

Currently we only need the following scopes:

- [links:read] for `lightdash.cloud` : Links to `lightdash.cloud` will be sent to our App
  so we can make an internal request and fetch information from charts and dashboards so we can later share it in slack.
- [links:write] : This allow us to unfurl the URL previously mentioned.

No other content will be shared from your workspace.

  src={SlackOauth}

  style={{ display: 'block', margin: '0 auto 20px auto' }}
/>

That's it

automatically unfurled with your chart or dashboard details.
